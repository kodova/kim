Why is tuna salad so popular?!

I can only assume the simplicity of tuna salad is the main reason anyone makes it.  It doesn't have great texture, the flavor is simple, the color is almost disgusting, and although it is called a salad - is really isn't.

What if I told you that there is another option for Tuna Salad that has better texture and flavor but is still simple to make?  Oh, and it might be healthier too!  This recipe is similar in idea to my Curried Egg Salad</a>, they both have bolder flavor and more texture than the original.

This Thai Tuna Salad adds beans for texture and to make it more filling.  It has a simple sauce using soy (or tamari) and peanut butter!  The flavor is strong enough that if you have kids that don't like tuna it masks some of the flavor.  And the cherry tomatoes add great color.  You could also use other vegetables instead of tomatoes, like bell peppers or even broccoli.  Whatever you enjoy.

KIM Tips

  • For the best texture, make sure you drain and rinse your beans!</li>
  • If you refrigerate your peanut butter, you may need to heat it up in order to mix the sauce together.</li>
