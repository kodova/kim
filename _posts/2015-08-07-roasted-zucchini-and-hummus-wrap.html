I eat a lot of salads for lunch as it is an easy way for me to get in my vegetables.  But sometimes I need a little bit more, and simply wrapping it up in a tortilla can be sufficient.  The awesome thing about wrapping it up, you open the door to adding different vegetables and spread, like hummus, that you normally don't put in a salad.

For this wrap, I used the extra zucchini I roasted for dinner in my One Pan Oven Chicken Parmesan</a>.  You could use summer squash, and if you choose you could grill it instead of roasting in the oven.

You could use any hummus</a>, but here I chose an artichoke hummus which gave it a nice tangy mild flavor.
