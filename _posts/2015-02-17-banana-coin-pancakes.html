Happy Pancake Day!!!  Yesterday I gave you an Instruction Manual on Pancakes</a> that gave you a pancake mix recipe so you are prepared to have pancakes on any given day.  Because you have the mix ready, you can start experimenting with mix ins and toppings.

These pancakes have banana rounds in the center are lightly caramelized.  All you do is place a thin slice of banana in your pan, then cover it with pancake batter and continue as normal.  You can top it with maple syrup, toasted walnuts, cinnamon, or more bananas.

Kids love these because they are mini pancakes and can easily be eaten in one hand, and I have yet to find a kid that doesn't like bananas.  We buy a bunch of bananas nearly every time we are at the grocery store.
