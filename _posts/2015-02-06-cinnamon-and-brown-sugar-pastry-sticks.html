In my quest for easier homemade breakfasts I thought maybe I would try to make Cinnamon-Brown Sugar pop tarts.  I thought  I could make a batch over the weekend and have them in the freezer for a quick morning breakfast.

I quickly realized I didn't have the patience to roll and cut pastry dough into perfect thin rectangles.  My dough was sticky and no matter how much extra flour or extra chill time I gave it, it still stuck to the counter and the rolling pin or just fell apart when trying to move it.  Instead of trying to fix the problem, I decided it would be quicker to change up what I was making.

So I rolled out the dough, brushed with egg wash and sprinkled the cinnamon sugar all over the top.  Then cut out into strips.

These taste delicious, more like a dessert than breakfast! Does that make you think twice about handing over a pop tart to your kids when you are in a rush in morning?

Another positive is that these are better for portioning.  You can serve one or two on the side of eggs as special breakfast treat.  Pop tarts are served two in a bag, which for more kids is way more than they need.  But they will eat whatever you give them because most kids LOVE pop tarts.  If you make these sticks, you can give them a proper portion.

Forget feeding the kids... Scoop out some ice cream tonight and dig in.
