We all have a go to salad of veggies that are easy to keep in the pantry.  It is time to mix it up and start introducing new salads to your repertoire so you never get sick of a healthy lunch.  This salad has a simple homemade vinaigrette, I used lime to mix it up from the standard homemade dressings but you could use lemon if that is what you have on hand.  It also has a brilliant pesto made with the leaves from your celery stalks.  I love the nose to tail movement of cooking with meat, so why not use the same principle and use all parts of your veggies?  Both the dressing and pesto can be made in advance so you can prepare over the weekend for a weeknight side or enjoy it for lunch several days in a row without too much work.

This paired beautifully with a glass of ice water with cucumber.  Drinking water with a salad was never so easy!
