One of my favorite desserts is apple crisps with ice cream.  However that is not something I should eat everyday!  This may not be an everyday breakfast either, but it is easy to keep the parts around and throw together on a morning that you need something extra to get going, or for a special breakfast over the weekend.

My kids love waffles.  So this is a great way to give them a breakfast that they will eat all of to keep them going throughout the morning.  Plus it has a serving a fruits!

There is no recipe here!  It is simply composed of a toasted waffle (I use Van's Power Grains or Gluten Free Ancient Grains), a spoonful of my Apple Pie Topping</a> (that can be frozen for months) and sprinkled with Granola.  You could use my homemade Cinnamon-Cranberry Granola</a> or store bought.  However, try to choose one that has cinnamon as that will give you the right flavors of an apple crisp.
