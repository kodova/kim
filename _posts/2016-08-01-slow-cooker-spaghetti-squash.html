Do you enjoy cooking squash?  The difficulty of cutting it in half or the heat of the oven for nearly an hour.

Yeah, I didn't think so!

What if I told you there was a way that you didn't have to cut the squash or deal with the heat?  It is true!  You can throw a whole squash in your slow cooker and let it go, untouched for a couple hours.  Then you have a perfectly cooked spaghetti squash with strands that unravel easily and are tender but cooked through.

KIM Tips

  • This method works for butternut squash as well!</li>
  • Your cook times may vary depending on the size of your squash</li>
  • If you are batch cooking or meal planning</a> this is a perfect item to be cooking while you are preparing other meals.</li>
