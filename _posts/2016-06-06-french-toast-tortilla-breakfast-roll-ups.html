This recipe is an amazing twist or hack on two breakfast classics - French Toast and Crepes.  The hack? I use whole grain tortillas!

You can fill these with your favorite french toast topping or crepes filling.  Use your imagination!  I chose to use yogurt</a> and strawberries (any berry or fruit would work).

Like any other roll up, this is kid-friendly.  It is easy and clean.  Set out filling and topping options and let them choose and assemble the roll ups.  This will get your kids excited about eating and hopefully about helping more in the kitchen.

KIM Tip:

  • Use a little yogurt on the edge of the tortilla to act as a sealant, keeping the roll together.</li>
  • If you want hot tortillas or are serving a large crowd, wrap the tortillas in foil as you go and store in an oven preheated to 200 degrees.</li>
