I am always trying new ways to be healthy.  Hummus is one of my staples, and I wanted to change it up... So I switched out the chickpeas for edamame and added some miso</a>.

I am declaring that any</em> version of Hummus is the new Ranch dip.  I have made it a priority to change the idea that if you want your kids to eat their veggies you need to serve them with Ranch to serving them with hummus.  So far it is working for me!  Adding variety to the flavor of hummus allows them (or me!) to not get bored with the dip.

This dip is so quick and easy.  You just throw the ingredients in a blender and whirl until smooth!   If you are missing either tahini or miso - you can just omit one or the other (not both).  You will get a different flavor profile, but still a good dip!
