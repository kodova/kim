As a stay at home mom, there are several days a week where I don't get to prepare breakfast for myself until it is almost time for lunch.  Even before kids, I would occasionally sleep in on the weekend and wake up hungry and the big debate between lunch and breakfast would occur.

The simplest way to come up with a brunch item is to take a vegetable you would prepare for dinner and add fried egg to it.  In this case - grilled asparagus.

</a>

A normal dinner side vegetable.  Top it with an egg, citrus, and parmesan cheese and you have brunch!
