Life is getting busy for my little family.  I am finding myself running around from one thing to the next. I am trying to figure out how to have homemade lunches that are vegetable centered, rather than sandwiches or quesadillas everyday.

This salad comes together in just minutes and requires little prep work - just a minute or two of slicing and a couple minutes to gather and mix up the ingredients for the sauce.

If you know you are going to serve this cold, you can skip the cooking of the chicken and save more time! Just leave out the additional oil (and garlic if you don't like raw garlic).

If you have little ones that don't like broccoli because of the taste or texture, this is a great introduction.  It doesn't look like broccoli and the taste is very mild.  Oh, and did I mention it is a nut butter sauce?!  If kids could have their vegetables slathered in anything it would probably be peanut butter or ranch, am I right?

You can store this salad for 3-5 days.  So I encourage you to make it the night before when you have little distractions or temptations that will make you change your mind!
