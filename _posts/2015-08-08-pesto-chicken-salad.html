This recipe is simpler than it initially may seem!  You can cook chicken specifically for this, or you can used leftover or rotisserie chicken.  Simply cut and shred the chicken into small bite sized pieces then mix in as few as 2 ingredients!  You can skip either the greek yogurt or the mayo, as well as the green onion.  However, I found the combination in the recipe below is a good mixture of flavor (a little tang from the yogurt) and texture (crunch from the onion).

You can use any pesto you like.  A traditional basil, store bought or homemade</a>, or even my kale pesto</a>.

Use this Pesto Chicken Salad in a toasted sandwich, in addition to a grilled cheese, on a bed of greens, or in a wrap!
