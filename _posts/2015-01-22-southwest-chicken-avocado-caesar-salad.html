The fun isn't over with my Slow Cooker Mexican Chicken</a>!  This is a southwestern inspired salad with a creamy avocado dressing that is similar to a caesar dressing, but healthier!  The avocado is blended up to create a creamy texture that replaces the mayo found in traditional caesar dressing.

I eat half an avocado nearly every day and they are a staple in my house to feed the little ones with.  They are nutrient dense with more than 20 vitamins and minerals, have healthy fats, and they are naturally sodium and cholesterol free!  It is soft and creamy and a great first food for babies.  Green foods scare a lot of kids, but who doesn't like avocados?  So get them used to eating green with avocado and then move on to something a little more flavorful.

Most days I eat smashed avocado on toast, topped with eggs and hot sauce.  That breakfast is like pizza for me, I can eat it almost every day and not get sick of it.  But I like to cook new recipes too much to actually eat the same thing every day!

If you are trying to put together a salad in under 5 minutes, then put together the dressing and top it with pico de gallo</a> and any leftover tortilla strips from the tortilla soup</a>.  If you have another 5 minutes, then create a heartier topping with corn and black beans (instructions below).
