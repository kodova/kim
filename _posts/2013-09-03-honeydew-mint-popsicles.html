A great way to save a lot of fruits and vegetables is to freeze them.  However, melons do not have a great texture after being frozen and thawed so it is necessary to transform them.  Popsicles are a perfect way to use up melon that you aren't going to eat fresh and to extend the melon season as it comes to an end (around October, depending on where you live).

As you can see in the picture above, it is also a nutritious cold treat to help your teething toddler!  This popsicle is a great way to introduce the often over-powering flavor of mint as well.
