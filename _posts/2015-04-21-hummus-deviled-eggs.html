Deviled eggs are a staple at most of my family gatherings.  It is hard to restrain myself and eat just one or two.  However, there is no need to restrain from these!  In fact, you can feel good about making these a regular snack.

We all know that eggs are a good source of protein.  But the mayo that is in most deviled eggs is loaded with fat and calories.  So I replaced the mayo with Roasted Red Pepper Hummus</a>.  Hummus is full of protein, it is also a good source of fiber and good-for-you carbs.

Like my Peanut Butter dip</a>, this is also just two ingredients!  Although I provide a recipe, it is just a starting point.  Add as much or as little of your favorite hummus to the egg yolks until it tastes good to you.

KIM Tip:  I don't use any fancy or complicated method to get a perfect hard boiled egg.  Instead, I use an Egg Timer</a>.  Its as simple as putting the timer in the water with your eggs, bringing it to a boil, and cooking until the timer indicates that it is at a hard.
