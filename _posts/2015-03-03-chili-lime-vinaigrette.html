Hey All! I am still cleaning out my pantry</a>, and it has been fun and interesting.  I have not shared a lot of our dishes because its been a lot of leftovers or an array of mismatched foods that I wouldn't normally recommend putting togethe!  But I plan to wrap it up soon and share with you know the lessons I have learned about my own pantry and how I should be purchasing and consuming food.

In my freezer I found a "recipe ready" bag that I bought to use for a last minute meal, but found that it contained ingredients I always kept on hand (corn, peppers, onion, and black beans) so I never really found it useful and it has been in the freezer for months.  I sautéed the vegetables and some chunks of chicken and put it all in leftover pita pockets from my Pita Pocket Greek Omelettes</a>.  I took the leftovers and made a salad with this chili-lime dressing.  It was made with limes I had juiced and frozen in ice cubes as well as a bunch of spices.  To learn more about salad dressings see my post "Instruction Manual: Salad Dressing</a>".

This vinaigrette would also be good with a salad such as my Southwest Chicken Salad</a> instead of the avocado caesar dressing.
