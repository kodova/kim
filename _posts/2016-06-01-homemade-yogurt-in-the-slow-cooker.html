Some days I struggle to find a use for some left over food (a big part of my meal planning</a>), other days ideas fall into my lap.

A couple weekends ago the Grandparents took the kids away for 4 days! We had just bought two gallons of milk and opened one.  The likelihood that I would drink the leftover milk before it went bad was very slim but it wasn't on my mind that I needed to find a way to use it up.  Instead, I was searching for some freezer and slow cooker meals that I could put together while the kids were out of town to help on those busy days that I don't have a lot of time to cook.  During that search, the idea of homemade yogurt in the slow cooker just fell in my lap.  Best of all, it uses half gallon of milk that I needed to use up!

KIM Tip</strong>
